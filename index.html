How do I know if my case is recoverable?
+

Every case is unique. We offer free consultations to evaluate your situation. Factors include the type of fraud, time elapsed, amount involved, and available documentation. Our success rate is 94% for cases we accept.

How long does the recovery process take?
+

Recovery timelines vary based on complexity. Simple exchange issues may resolve in 2-4 weeks, while complex fraud cases can take 2-6 months. We keep you informed throughout the entire process.

What are your fees?
+

We operate on a "No Win, No Fee" basis. You only pay a success fee when we recover your funds. The initial consultation is completely free. Fee percentages are discussed transparently before we begin.

What documents do I need?

Typically we need: transaction records, communication with the platform/scammer, wallet addresses involved, any contracts or agreements, and proof of identity. We'll guide you through exactly what's needed for your case.

Do you work internationally?

Yes, we operate globally. Our team has experience working with exchanges, regulators, and legal systems across Europe, North America, Asia, and beyond. Cryptocurrency fraud is borderless, and so is our recovery capability.
